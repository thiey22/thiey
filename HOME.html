KAKUMA REFUGEE CAMP SINCE 1992

Kakuma Refugee Camp was ertablished in the year 1992 with the displaced community from south Sudan due to heavy war in the southern part of the country, LOki was the main reception area of the new arrival before the establishment of the kakuma Refugee Camp in the year 1992. Today Kakuma is made up of different Nationalitie that flee from their country due several reasons. Here are the following Natoinalities in the camp, South Sudanese, Sudanese, Nigerian, Ugandan, Congolese, Ethiopian, Rwandanese, Burundian and Somalian.
